## Tips
- Highlights are **case-insensitive**: "Test" matches "test", "TEST", etc.
- Highlights match **whole words only**: "test" won't highlight "testing"
- Highlights are **saved automatically** in your browser
- Use different colors for different categories (e.g., Yellow for actions, Red for risks, Green for opportunities)

## Color Suggestions
- **Yellow**: Important points, key decisions
- **Red**: Risks, issues, blockers
- **Green**: Completed items, approvals
- **Blue**: Action items, tasks
- **Purple**: Questions, follow-ups
- **Orange**: Deadlines, time-sensitive items
- **Pink**: Names of people
- **Cyan**: Technical terms
- **Lime**: Positive feedback
- **Teal**: Data, metrics

## Common Words to Highlight
- Action words: "action", "todo", "task", "follow-up"
- Decision words: "decided", "approved", "rejected"
- Time words: "deadline", "urgent", "asap", "today"
- People: Names of key participants
- Projects: Project names or codes
- Status: "complete", "pending", "blocked", "in-progress"
